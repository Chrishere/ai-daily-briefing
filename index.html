来源：lilianweng.github.io · Scaling Laws, Carefully（6 月 24 日）

The observation is simple in form: the training loss L decreases predictably as we scale up model size N, dataset size D, and compute C, following a power-law curve. Given a fixed compute budget, it is more efficient to train a very large model and stop before convergence than to train a smaller model all the way to convergence.
