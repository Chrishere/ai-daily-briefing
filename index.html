Both CAISI and ECI paint an incomplete picture, as both use standardized (and simple) setups to compare the capabilities of models. To be more concrete: Coding tasks are evaluated using access to bash and a for-loop with a fixed budget of tokens, not with a harness such as Claude Code or OpenCode, which models are trained in! The mouse pointer has been a constant companion on computer screens, across every website, document and workflow. Despite how technologies have changed, the pointer has barely evolved in more than half a century. Our goal is to address a common frustration: because a typical AI tool lives in its own window, users need to drag their world into it. We want the opposite: intuitive AI that meets users across all the tools they use, without interrupting their flow.
