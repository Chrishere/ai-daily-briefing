总结：Hugging Face 宣布实现 ARD（Agentic Resource Discovery）规范——一个开放标准，允许 AI Agent 在运行时动态搜索并接入工具、技能或其他 Agent，而无需预先安装所有依赖。ARD 将能力筛选从 LLM 本身移到外部注册中心，注册信息包含发布者身份、合规认证等丰富信号，实现更可靠的工具发现。该标准对任何公司开放实现，旨在成为下一代 AI Agent 互操作生态系统的重要基础设施。

ARD moves selection outside the LLM. A registry indexes capabilities with richer signals such as publisher identity, representative queries, compliance attestations, and tags. It is not a product or a marketplace. It is a shared standard that any company can implement independently, and that any agent or tool can participate in.
查看中文翻译
