Latent Space 技术指南：RL 训练环境故障率超 5% 就是 Harness 问题，不是模型问题

来源：latent.space · How to Stop Shipping Low-Quality RL Environments (with Examples)（6 月 5 日）
总结：Latent Space 刊载 Auriel W 的深度 RL 工程指南，核心论点：RL 环境本质是"数据生成器"，一个不稳定的 harness 会把垃圾数据直接灌入训练步骤，系统性污染学习信号。文章给出明确阈值：环境故障率超过 5%，问题根源在 harness 而非模型本身。优质 harness 有正向复利效应，每次干净的 episode 都在积累；劣质 harness 同样有复利效应，只是方向相反——且往往难以察觉，直到造成不可挽回的训练损耗。

The environment is your data generator. A flaky harness systematically generates garbage data and feeds it straight into your model's learning steps. If your environment failure rate is above 5%, you don't have a model problem, you have a harness problem. A good harness compounds: every clean episode builds on the last. A bad one compounds too, just in the wrong direction.
